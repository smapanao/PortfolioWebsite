I'm a 25 year old (born September 23,1996) currently living in Mississauga, Ontario. I graduated highschool at St. Marguerite D'Youville secondary school in 2014. Following graduation, I spent one year at York University, but dropped out after one semester. In 2017, I decided to go back to school and attended Humber College for the Protection, Security, and Investigations program where I graduated with honors and was placed on the Dean's list in 2018.

After a year of work, I decided to change my field and am now a current student of Sheridan College's Software Development and Network Engineering program based in Oakville, Ontario. In the upcoming years I hope to strengthen my programming abilities and eventually find a job in the field.

I originally created this website as coding practice, but plan on occasionally mapping out my thoughts and feelings in a blog styled fashion, as well as updating my experience section as I continue to create side projects.
